# Phase 20 — User SOP (Vendor)

Audience: vendors. Surface: the vendor job-detail page (`/vendor/jobs/<assignment>`), Photos section.

## Attaching a photo to a work order

1. Open your assignment, scroll to **Photos**.
2. Give the photo a **title** (required — e.g. "Compressor nameplate before service").
3. **Add the photo** (optional):
   - On a **phone**, the file picker offers the **camera** directly (take the photo on the spot) or pick
     from your library.
   - On a **desktop**, choose an image file.
   - Accepted formats: **JPG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC** — up to **15 MB**.
4. Tap **Attach photo**.
   - With a photo selected, it's uploaded and shown as a thumbnail in the Photos list.
   - With **no** photo selected, a **title-only placeholder** is recorded (the prior behavior — useful to
     note "photo to follow").

> If you see "Unsupported file type" use JPG/PNG/WEBP/HEIC; "File too large" means it's over 15 MB
> (retake at lower resolution); "Upload failed, please try again" is a transient storage error — retry.

## Viewing photos

The Photos list shows each attachment with the uploader and time. Uploaded photos render as a
**thumbnail** — tap it to open the full image (the link is valid for a few minutes; reopen the page to
refresh it). Title-only entries show a **Placeholder** tag.

## What this does NOT do

- Photos you upload are **internal to the aggregator** — they are **not** automatically shown to the
  client. An operator decides what gets shared.
- There is no separate "documents" upload here yet — this is the work-order photo surface.
